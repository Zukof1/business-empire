# 🏢 Business Empire

An interactive business management simulator built with Three.js and modern JavaScript.

### 🚀 Play Now
**[Click here to play Business Empire](https://zukof1.github.io/business-empire/)**

---

### 🌟 Latest Features
* **Business Star Upgrades:** New interactive Three.js visual feedback for business growth.
* **Prestige System:** Reset for powerful multipliers and permanent unlocks.
* **Garage & Dealership:** Manage your luxury assets and vehicle collection.
* **Stock Market:** Real-time trading with multi-buy toggles and advanced UI.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
* **Graphics:** Three.js for visual feedback
* **Automation/Testing:** Playwright & Puppeteer
